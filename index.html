Process

Intersectional Interests

My usual process combines a love of hard deadlines with design thinking.



My approach to design thinking is often influenced by four backgrounds:

3D Experience Design

My first love is producing an experience of blended forms. 3D fully uses my practice in product, performance, teaching, game design, video, and journalism.

Solutions Architecture

"When has there been a similar problem to this in the past?" Analogizing new challenges to past cases makes solutions ideation easier.

Intuitive Communication

Measuring intuitivity is grounded in measuring how effectively your teaching facilitates comprehension. The solutions are different per medium, but the benchmark is the same: "Do people get it?"

Collaborative Management

If you want to avoid blindspots, you need to get different perspectives. Go beyond the team - users, beginners, bartenders. Never design by committee, but surprising solutions come from surprising places.
